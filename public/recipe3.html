THIRSTY! A LEMONADE IS ALL YOU NEED

1. Cut your lemon in half. 2. Squeeze all the juice out of it that you can. 3. Pour your juice into a container with 1/4 cup (2 oz) sugar. 4. Add a very small amount of water to your container. 5. Stir your solution until sugar dissolves. 6. Fill up container with water and add ice. 7. Put your lemonade in the fridge for five minutes. see a pic of the lemonade then go drink one.
